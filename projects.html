Online Quiz Application

The 'MCQ Quiz Application' project streamlines tests by reducing manual labor and requiring low computing knowledge. Hospital Finder

Hospital Finder is a mobile app designed to assist individuals in navigating healthcare facilities during medical emergencies. It offers a user-friendly platform, a review system, and direct appointment scheduling, enhancing decision-making and minimizing wait times for patients and healthcare providers.
